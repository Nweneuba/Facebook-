THE FOUNDER

Dr.Gilbert Onyekachukwu Ibezim born 19 November 1978 is a Nigerian politician and a medical doctor who is the current Deputy Governor of Anambra State since 17 March 2022.he won the 2021 Anambra State gubernatorial election alongside Governor Charles Soludo on the platform of the All progressive Grand Alliance(APGA).Onyeka is married with children,from a native of Mbaukwu,Awka south local Government Area, Anambra state he is also a brother to His Grace, The Most Revd Alexander Chibuzor Ibezim Phd,Archbishop on the Niger provice and Bishop of Awka diocese,He attended Federal government college,Minna. He proceeded to study medicine at Nnamdi Azikiwe University, Nnewi campus.

Dr.Onyekachukwu Ibezim started his career as a medical doctor. He worked with the Nigerian ministry of defence. in 2014, Onyeka Ibezim joined polictics and was appointed the special adviser to the Governor on youth empowernment and Executive Director of Anambra State AIDs control Agency by Governor Willie Obiano. in 2018, he was appointed the special adviser to the Governor on traditional medicine and herbal practice after Governor Obiano won his re-election bid.in 2021, Onyeka Ibezim was picked as the running mate of Charles Soludo in Anambra general election under the platform of All Progressive Grand Alliance(APGA).he was elected as the Anambra State Deputy Governor in November 2021 under the All Progressive Grand Alliance(APGA).
